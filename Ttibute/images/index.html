TAKESHI KOVACS

Takeshi Lev Kovacs (Hungarian pronunciation: [ˈkovaːt͡ʃ];from the Hungarian word for "[black]smith") is the protagonist of the books Altered Carbon, Broken Angels, and Woken Furies by Richard K. Morgan, which takeplace in or around the 26th century.
